Smart Routing Optimization

$2.05M annual cost savings by routing transactions to the optimal processor based on geography.

Timeline: Ongoing through 2026
Impact: $2.05M Annual
Status: Operational

! The Problem

  • Using primarily Nuvei for all markets
  • Nuvei rate: 1.5% IC++ (Interchange Plus Plus)
  • One-size-fits-all approach
  • Not optimized by geographic market

✓ Smart Routing Logic

  • US, Canada, Europe, ANZ go to Worldpay (IC+0.15%)
  • APAC (Japan, Korea, Thailand, Singapore, HK) go to Nuvei (IC+1.5%)
  • Worldpay: 48% cheaper for US/Europe
  • Nuvei: 41% cheaper for APAC

Initiative 4

Global 3DS2 Optimization

$853K recovered by switching from 3DS2 Direct to Split Authentication, rescuing Japan and Europe approval rates.

Timeline: Jan - Aug 2025
Impact: $853K Annual
Status: Complete

! Root Cause

WorldPay 3DS2 Direct sent ALL transactions for authorization, including failed authentications. Banks declined heavily due to poor authentication results. Japan crashed from 89% to 55%. Europe stuck at 44%.

✓ Split Authentication Model

Separate authentication from authorization. Evaluate the authentication result BEFORE sending to the bank. Only forward clean, trustworthy transactions.

IF authentication fails: IF (device fingerprint present AND billing address present AND browser info present AND ECI = 05 or 02): ALLOW authorization ELSE: BLOCK transaction

The Bottleneck Question

MID Application Context

⏳ What Controls the Timeline

MID (Merchant ID) applications require specific Finance documentation: incorporation documents, banking letters (less than 30 days old), financial statements, and tax documentation. Processor underwriting takes 2-4 weeks once docs are complete. The bottleneck is Finance documentation preparation, not processor relationships.

While MIDs were pending, I was recovering $4.6M in US market, optimizing approval rates globally, saving $2.05M in processing costs, and preventing $1.17M in chargebacks.

I focus on what I can control. And I deliver results.
