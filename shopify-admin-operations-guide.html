üè∑Ô∏è Discounts
Admin ‚Ä∫ Discounts
Create and manage discount codes and automatic discounts. Shopify supports four discount types. No discounts have been created yet.
Types Available Discount Types
üí∞
Amount Off Products
Percentage or fixed amount off specific products or collections. Can target all customers, specific segments, or individual customers. Supports code-based or automatic application.
Options
Method: Discount code or Automatic. Value: Percentage or fixed. Applies to: Specific collections or all products. Eligibility: All, segments, or specific customers. Min purchase: None, amount, or quantity. Usage limits: Total uses, one per customer. Combinations: Can toggle product, order, shipping discount stacking. Active dates: Start/end with time.
üéÅ
Buy X Get Y
Customer buys a minimum quantity or amount, gets items at a discount. "Customer buys" and "Customer gets" sections with separate product/collection targeting. Discount can be percentage, fixed amount off each, or free.
Options
Buy trigger: Min quantity or min purchase amount from specific products. Get reward: Quantity of specific products at percentage off, amount off, or free. Can set max uses per order.
üßæ
Amount Off Order
Percentage or fixed amount off the entire order total. Simpler than product discounts. Good for sitewide sales or loyalty rewards.
Options
Same eligibility, minimums, usage limits, and combination controls as product discounts. Applies to the whole cart rather than specific items.
üöö
Free Shipping
Removes shipping costs for qualifying orders. Can limit to specific countries or all countries. Can exclude orders over a shipping rate threshold.
Options
Countries: All or selected. Shipping rates: Option to exclude rates over a certain amount. Supports all standard eligibility, minimums, and combination controls. Can combine with product and order discounts (not shipping).
Methods Discount Code vs Automatic
üîë
Discount Code
Customer enters a code at checkout. You create the code or let Shopify generate a random one. Good for targeted promotions, influencer partnerships, and email campaigns.
‚ö°
Automatic Discount
Applied automatically when conditions are met. No code needed. Customer sees the discount in cart. Good for sitewide sales and BOGO offers where you want zero friction.
Combinations Discount Stacking Rules
By default, discounts cannot combine with other discounts. You can enable stacking by checking these boxes when creating a discount:
Product Discounts
Allow this discount to combine with product-level discounts.
Order Discounts
Allow this discount to combine with order-level discounts.
Shipping Discounts
Allow this discount to combine with shipping discounts. Not available on Free Shipping type.
üí°
Sales Channel Access: Each discount has a "Sales channel access" toggle. Check "Allow discount to be featured on selected channels" to make it visible on your Online Store and POS.
